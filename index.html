The bioinformatics compendium

Motivation

This is a bioinformatics compendium for the publication: Woodworth et al. A novel adjuvant formulation induces robust Th1/Th17 memory and mucosal recall responses in Non-Human Primates.

The authors strive to contribute to the scientific rigor, reproducibility, and transparency.

Thus, we provide this compendium, in order to:

  • Document a fully reproducible pipeline

  • Facilitate the readers to freely access the data, statistical models, analysis
